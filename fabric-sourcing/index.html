Fabric Sourcing for Apparel Brands Capabilities

Fabric sourcing is more useful when it is connected to garment construction, MOQ, shrinkage, hand feel and production feasibility. Cyncho helps buyers evaluate materials with the finished garment in mind.

We support cotton, silk, wool, organic cotton, knits, linings and accessories for fabrics and trims for womenswear production, helping brands avoid material choices that create production problems later.

  • Review fabrics and trims for womenswear production before quotation so production expectations are clear.
  • Coordinate cotton, silk, wool, organic cotton, knits, linings and accessories during development, sampling and production planning.
  • Support brands that need sourcing connected to real garment production with practical factory communication and export-ready details.

Why Global Brands Work With Cyncho Garments

Cyncho Garments supports fashion brands that need a reliable apparel manufacturer in China for sampling, OEM/ODM production, fabric and trim sourcing, and custom development. Our workflow helps buyers move from idea to production with clearer communication.

For women’s apparel, private label collections, or seasonal fashion programs, our team helps align product quality, lead time, and export-ready documentation from the start.

What products do you manufacture?

We focus on women’s apparel, including tops, dresses, jackets, knitwear, bottoms, and jumpsuits, plus related sourcing and development support.

Can you help with sampling first?

Yes. We can move from tech pack or reference sample to fitting sample, comments, and production approval before bulk production starts.

Do you support fabric sourcing?

Yes. We help source fabrics, trims, and accessories so the final product stays consistent with your design and cost target.

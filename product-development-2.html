Product Development

Have you ever wondered how much effort goes into creating a single piece of clothing? Let us take you behind the scenes of our garment production process – it’s more intricate than you might think!

Design & Development Stage
Designer Drafting**: Our designers create sketches based on the latest market trends. Fabric & Trims Selection**: We carefully source fabrics, buttons, zippers, and other materials from the market. Pattern Making & Sample Creation**: Pattern makers transform sketches into 3D paper patterns and produce the first sample garment. Modification & Finalization**: Multiple fittings and adjustments ensure the perfect fit and style. High-end brands often revise designs more than 10 times!*

Production Preparation Stage
Fabric Pre-Shrinking Treatment: Washing or steaming fabrics to prevent shrinkage in the finished product. Layout & Cutting: Using CAD systems for intelligent fabric layout, and laser cutting machines for precise cutting. Sorting & Packaging: Cut fabric pieces are sorted and prepared for the assembly line.

Sewing & Processing Stage
Assembly Line Work: Over 20 specialized steps including stitching, sleeve attachment, and edge locking. Special Techniques: Printing, embroidery, washing, and distressing effects add unique details. In-Process Quality Checks: QC teams inspect every stage to ensure high quality.

Post-processing Stage
Pressing and Finishing: High-temperature steam ensures garments are smooth and wrinkle-free Final Inspection and Quality Control: Checking for loose threads, stains, and dimensional discrepancies Labeling and Packaging: Attaching labels, placing in dust-proof bags, and packing into boxes

The Final Line of Defense Before Shipment Random Sampling and Testing: Some items must pass third-party quality inspections (must comply with GB18401 national standards) Packing and Shipping: Ship to brand partners or e-commerce warehouses according to orders

About Us

Dongguan CYNCHO Garment Factory was established in 2010, is a small sewing machine workshop slowly evolved, now the factory is equipped with the number of people 30-50 people, 40 parking spaces, the annual output of more than 300,000 pieces, At present, the company has a wholly-owned woven factory, specializing in the production of all kinds of woven women's and men's apparel, like top, shirt, pant,dress, jacket,coat, There is also an invested woolen factory that produces all kinds of knitted and sweater clothes. Our factory has a complete set of complete production system, through the SEDEX and GOTS GRS RWS certification, hope to be able to a new look of quality service and high-end quality to better serve our customers.

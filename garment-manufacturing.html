Garment Manufacturing

Welcome to the world of Garment Manufacturing – where creativity meets precision and craftsmanship.

At Cyncho Garments Pvt. Ltd. , we take pride in weaving dreams into reality through our exceptional garment manufacturing services. With an unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability, we transform fabrics into masterpieces that celebrate individuality and style.

Cyncho has always been committed to serving the mid-to-high-end segment of the global fashion industry

1. Imagination Collection – Your Ideas, Our Expertise
We begin by gathering your valuable input—whether it’s sketches, original samples, or detailed ideas. Our team works closely with you to capture every detail, including measurements, tech packs, and any special requests. This thorough preparation ensures our pattern designers have all the information necessary to create accurate and flawless patterns that reflect your vision.

2. Expert Sample Pattern Creation
Once we receive the finished tech packs and fabrics, our highly experienced pattern designers—each with over 20 years of industry expertise—start crafting your sample patterns. Their skill and attention to detail guarantee that the samples are both true to your design and optimized for production, minimizing errors and accelerating your product development timeline.

3. Sustainable Bulk Fabric Selection
Selecting the right fabric is crucial. We curate and recommend sustainable fabrics that not only meet your quality standards but also align with eco-friendly practices. Our vast network of textile suppliers allows us to source materials that fulfill your design requirements, durability needs, and ethical production goals.

4. Sewing & Production:
Once all the fabric is precisely cut, each piece of clothing moves into the sewing phase. Our skilled workers, each with over 20 years of industry experience, handle the sewing with utmost care and expertise. They treat every garment like a precious baby, ensuring that every stitch and seam meets the highest standards. Their dedication and craftsmanship are reflected in the durability and aesthetic appeal of our finished products.

5. Quality Assurance
We maintain strict control over every step in the production process. Each garment undergoes rigorous quality checks between 5 to 6 times before it is approved for shipping. This careful inspection guarantees that the final product not only meets but exceeds customer expectations. Our commitment to quality ensures consistent excellence and reliability in every piece we deliver.

6. After-Sales Service
Our relationship with customers does not end at delivery. We pride ourselves on our 24-hour after-sales service, providing quick and responsive support to address any inquiries or concerns. Fast replies and effective solutions are our mission because your satisfaction matters most to us.

In summary, from the skilled hands of our experienced team to the stringent quality control measures and dedicated after-sales support, we strive to provide you with garments that embody quality, comfort, and style. We look forward to the opportunity to partner with you and meet your production needs with excellence.

What Garment Manufacturing Support Includes

Our garment manufacturing service is built for brands that need production-ready support from a China apparel factory. We help turn approved samples into finished orders with attention to fit, material consistency, and export requirements.

Can you handle small and medium orders?

We can support orders depending on the product, fabric, and production schedule, so please share your target quantity in the inquiry.

What is the normal production flow?

The flow is request, sampling, approval, production, quality check, and shipment. This gives buyers a clear path from idea to finished goods.

Do you support quality inspection?

Yes. Quality control is part of production support so the final goods match the approved sample as closely as possible.

About Us

Dongguan CYNCHO Garment Factory was established in 2010, is a small sewing machine workshop slowly evolved, now the factory is equipped with the number of people 30-50 people, 40 parking spaces, the annual output of more than 300,000 pieces, At present, the company has a wholly-owned woven factory, specializing in the production of all kinds of woven women's and men's apparel, like top, shirt, pant,dress, jacket,coat, There is also an invested woolen factory that produces all kinds of knitted and sweater clothes. Our factory has a complete set of complete production system, through the SEDEX and GOTS GRS RWS certification, hope to be able to a new look of quality service and high-end quality to better serve our customers.

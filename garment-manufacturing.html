Garment Manufacturing

Welcome to the world of Garment Manufacturing – where creativity meets precision and craftsmanship.

At Cyncho Garments Pvt. Ltd. , we take pride in weaving dreams into reality through our exceptional garment manufacturing services. With an unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability, we transform fabrics into masterpieces that celebrate individuality and style.

Cyncho has always been committed to serving the mid-to-high-end segment of the global fashion industry

Our Garment Manufacturing Process:

1. Imagination Unleashed:
Every garment starts as a vision, an idea that holds the potential to inspire. Our team of skilled designers works closely with clients to understand their unique vision, combining creativity and technical expertise to craft designs that speak volumes.

2. Precision in Pattern Making:
Before a fabric is cut and stitched, precise patterns are created to ensure a perfect fit and flawless construction. Our pattern makers meticulously translate designs into blueprints that form the foundation of each garment, guaranteeing the highest level of accuracy.

3. Fabric Selection:
We believe that the choice of fabric defines the character of a garment. From sumptuous silks to comfortable cottons, we handpick the finest materials from trusted suppliers, promoting sustainable practices whenever possible

4. Craftsmanship & Tailoring:
Our skilled artisans and seamstresses breathe life into the fabrics, employing age-old techniques alongside modern technology to bring out the best in each garment. Every stitch is infused with dedication and attention to detail, ensuring a beautiful finish.

5. Quality Assurance:
We never compromise on quality. Each garment undergoes rigorous quality checks at various stages of production to meet the highest standards. Our commitment to excellence guarantees that only the best reaches our clients

6. Ethical and Sustainable:
At Cyncho Garments Pvt. Ltd., sustainability is at the heart of what we do. We promote ethical practices, fair wages, and eco-friendly production methods, striving to minimize our environmental footprint while creating stunning fashion.

7. Customization and Flexibility:
We understand that every client is unique, and so are their requirements. Our team works closely with you to provide tailored solutions that meet your specific needs, from small-batch productions to larger scale orders.

8. Timely Delivery:
We value your time as much as our own. With efficient production processes and well-organized logistics, we ensure timely delivery without compromising on quality.

About Us

Dongguan CYNCHO Garment Factory was established in 2010, is a small sewing machine workshop slowly evolved, now the factory is equipped with the number of people 30-50 people, 40 parking spaces, the annual output of more than 300,000 pieces, At present, the company has a wholly-owned woven factory, specializing in the production of all kinds of woven women's and men's apparel, like top, shirt, pant,dress, jacket,coat, There is also an invested woolen factory that produces all kinds of knitted and sweater clothes. Our factory has a complete set of complete production system, through the SEDEX and GOTS GRS RWS certification, hope to be able to a new look of quality service and high-end quality to better serve our customers.

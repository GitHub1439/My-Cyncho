Garment Designing

We believe that the process of designing clothing and bringing it to life is what brings us closest together.

Research & Conceptualization
Market Research: We conduct in-depth analyses of industry trends, target consumer characteristics, trending colors, and fabric & accessory developments to ensure market relevance. - Theme Selection: Inspiration is drawn from art, nature, daily life, and travel to define a cohesive and compelling apparel collection theme. - Mood Board Creation: Gathering textual materials, imagery, color palettes, and fabric samples, we create a visual Mood Board that solidifies the color schemes, fabric choices, and silhouette concepts of the collection.

Design Development

Sketching: Rapid and rough sketches help transform inspiration into visual fashion forms, encouraging expansive and divergent thinking. - Final Selection: From numerous sketches, we select styles that align with both the theme and market demands, refining them in detail. - Design Documentation: We finalize fabric and accessory selections, color combinations, and design details (such as buttonholes, studs, sewing techniques), producing comprehensive technical sketches or renderings.

Technical Design & Prototyping
Pattern Making: Our experts translate two-dimensional design drawings into three-dimensional structural patterns, laying the foundation for sample creation and fitting accuracy.

About Us

Dongguan CYNCHO Garment Factory was established in 2010, is a small sewing machine workshop slowly evolved, now the factory is equipped with the number of people 30-50 people, 40 parking spaces, the annual output of more than 300,000 pieces, At present, the company has a wholly-owned woven factory, specializing in the production of all kinds of woven women's and men's apparel, like top, shirt, pant,dress, jacket,coat, There is also an invested woolen factory that produces all kinds of knitted and sweater clothes. Our factory has a complete set of complete production system, through the SEDEX and GOTS GRS RWS certification, hope to be able to a new look of quality service and high-end quality to better serve our customers.

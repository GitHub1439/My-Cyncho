What to Include in Your Inquiry

To receive a useful apparel manufacturing quote, include your product category, quantity, size range, fabric direction, target market, expected delivery timeline and any tech pack, sample photo or reference style. These details help Cyncho review OEM pricing, sampling options and production feasibility more accurately.

If you are comparing suppliers, you can also ask about MOQ, sample lead time, fabric sourcing, private label support and quality control steps before starting bulk production.

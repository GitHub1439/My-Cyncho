Common Inquiry Questions

If you are sending an inquiry for OEM, ODM, sampling, or sourcing support, these quick answers help you know what to include so we can respond faster.

What is your MOQ?

MOQ depends on style, fabric, construction, color count and order plan. Send your product details and target quantity so we can review a realistic low MOQ or small batch production option.

Do you offer OEM and ODM services?

Yes. We support OEM production from your tech pack, sample or specifications, and ODM development support when you need help with fabric, construction, sampling or production-ready adjustments.

Can you make samples before production?

Yes. Sample development is recommended before bulk production so we can confirm fit, fabric behavior, trims, workmanship and finishing details.

What countries do you ship to?

We can ship internationally and have experience supporting buyers in Australia, New Zealand, the United States, the United Kingdom, Germany and other overseas markets.

How long does production take?

For many straightforward styles, first samples take about 3 to 5 working days. Bulk production often takes about 15 to 20 days after sample approval, depending on quantity, fabric and complexity.

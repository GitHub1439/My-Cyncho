A guide for fashion brands looking for a private label clothing manufacturer, covering labels, trims, sampling, sourcing and production planning.

What Private Label Means

Private label clothing manufacturing allows you to produce apparel under your own brand with custom labels, trims and packaging details.

What to Prepare

Prepare your product references, brand label requirements, size range, quantity, fabric preferences and target customer.

Sampling Comes First

A sample helps confirm fit, fabric, trims and construction before bulk private label production begins.

Work With a Long-term Factory Partner

Private label brands benefit from a factory that understands repeat orders, seasonal updates and consistent quality expectations.

About Us

Dongguan CYNCHO Garment Factory was established in 2010, starting from a small sewing workshop and growing into a professional garment manufacturing partner. We operate a wholly-owned woven factory for womenswear and also work with an invested knitwear and sweater factory, supporting global fashion brands with fabric sourcing, product development, sampling, quality control and bulk apparel production.

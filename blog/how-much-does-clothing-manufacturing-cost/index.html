How Much Does Clothing Manufacturing Cost?

A practical guide to clothing manufacturing cost factors, including fabric, trims, quantity, sample development, labor and quality requirements.

Main Cost Factors

Cost depends on fabric, trims, construction complexity, order quantity, sample revisions, packaging and quality requirements.

Why MOQ Matters

Higher quantities usually improve unit cost, while small orders may carry higher material and production setup costs.

Sampling Cost vs Bulk Cost

Sampling is different from bulk production because it includes development time, pattern work, fabric testing and revisions.

How to Get an Accurate Quote

Prepare style images, tech packs, quantity, size range, fabric needs and target market before requesting pricing.

OEM vs ODM Clothing Manufacturing

Understand the difference between OEM and ODM clothing manufacturing and choose the right production model for your apparel brand.

What OEM Means

OEM clothing manufacturing means the factory produces garments based on your design, tech pack, specification and brand requirements.

What ODM Means

ODM support usually includes more design and development input from the manufacturer, helping brands move from concept to finished product faster.

Which One Should You Choose?

Choose OEM if you already have detailed designs. Choose ODM if you need stronger development support, sourcing advice or sample guidance.
